About US

IND RockerZ isn't just a band, it's a force of nature. Born from the shared passion of four individuals who refuse to be confined by musical boundaries, we're a sonic explosion of raw energy, infectious melodies, and unbridled passion.
Together, we create a sound that is both familiar and electrifying. We draw inspiration from the greats of rock, but we're not afraid to push the boundaries and forge our own path. Our music is a celebration of life, love, and the power of music to connect us all.
IND RockerZ is more than just a band. We're a movement. We're a sound. We're a force to be reckoned with.

Our RockerZ -

Justin Cook, our lead vocalist, commands the stage with a voice that can both soothe and shatter. His lyrics, raw and honest, delve into the depths of human experience, touching on themes of love, loss, and the relentless pursuit of dreams.

Kinder Cross, our lead guitarist, is a master of the six-string, weaving intricate riffs and soaring solos that leave audiences breathless. His guitar work is a whirlwind of technical prowess and emotional depth, pushing the boundaries of rock and roll.

Tim Timberlake, our bassist, provides the rhythmic backbone that anchors our sound. His grooves are both powerful and subtle, laying the foundation for the band's explosive energy and driving force.

Travis Scott, our drummer, is a powerhouse behind the kit. His relentless rhythms and thunderous fills are the heartbeat of IND RockerZ, driving the music forward with an unstoppable energy.
